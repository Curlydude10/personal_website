Who am I

Hello! Welcome to my website, I am glad you're here and hope you find something interesting and exciting! This website will has a couple of purposes: it is a place to share my projects, techncial deep dives on the math and theory behind engineering topics I find interesting, a digital resume, and finally playground for experimenting with visual design prinicpals.

At the time of writing I am not yet sure what projects I will embark on other than this website, but they will likely be a combination of hardware, FPGA, firmware, and software projects as that is my technical background and what I find interesting. I hope to begin with some power and SDR projects, but we shall see.

I also plan to use this as a place to share technical deep dives on the math and theory behind engineering topics I find interesting. I am hoping to write these deep dives as interactive technical experiments so that readers can get an understanding and intuition behind the theory of the deep dives. I hope that these can serve as helpful resources and inspiration for others seeking to try new things and learn through experimentation.

A bit about me: I am an adventurer, engineer, design nerd, and life long learner. I grew up in colorado and now spend my days designing electronics for satellites. I love learning about what life has to offer from the technical nuance to the esoteric and bizzare. And I hope for this project to be a way to share my excitment and curiousity about life. I am excited to see where it goes and to have you along for the ride.
